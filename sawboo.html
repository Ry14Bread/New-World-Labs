PRIMARY ANOMALY: THE C.P.N.

The Central Perspective Node (C.P.N.) is identified as the primary locus of consciousness within biological entities. It is typically located in the posterior brain region above the Thalamus.

The C.P.N. appears to function as a “filter node” between subjective awareness and external non-physical influence.
