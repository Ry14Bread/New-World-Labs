New World Labs Internal Classification

The C.P.N.

The Central Perspective Node is where a biological figure's consciousness is mainly located. For most of the time, it is located in the back of the brain, above the Thalamus.

Universe Model: Domus 001 // Universe A-OW(1)

Domus Structure Components:

  • The Core: Where physical matter and energy is located.
  • The Border Ring: Prevents matter from leaving or entering the Vivid Ring.
  • The Vivid Ring: Translates non-compliant matter and energy, enforcing new rules upon it. Appears to derive rules from interacting C.P.N. systems.
  • The Membrane: Separates this universe from external matter and energy systems.

Vivid Ring Neural Interaction

During a Vivid Ring contact event, the Thalamus, Hippocampus, and Amygdala form an under-conscious semi-neural pathway beneath the C.P.N., known as the Somniportascire Neural Pathway.

Somniportascire Pathway & Creatus-Somniportascire

When a biological consciousness interacts with the Vivid Ring—most commonly during dreaming— the Somniportascire Pathway is temporarily formed.

In most cases, this structure is removed after dreaming via hormonal regulation and immune response. However, in rare cases where it is not removed, it develops into a physical structure known as the Creatus-Somniportascire Foreman Lobe.

This structure acts as a limited “gate” to the Vivid Ring, allowing small amounts of energy and consciousness to pass through.
